ABOUT US
Full Local Bespoke Media Production 
Newland Studios is a new media company established in Hull in 2012. We specialise in bespoke media production taylored to every need whether it is a family occasion or a corporate presentation.

We offer a complete service from initial conception to Media copying. We offer a complete production planning service, studio and location shooting and  full post production facilities. 

We have covereded everything from Fashion Shows, Weddings, Live events, Music Promos, Industrial Processess, Plays and Pantomimes, as well as working with many commercial customers producing numerous instructional and promotional videos for our many and varied clients. 
. Full Broadcast Quality Video Production

. Full Pre-Production and Planning Service.

. Multi-Camera Live Event Service Available.

. Complete Post Production Service including Editing and DVD Production.
Commercial Productions
Newland Studios have worked with a number of Commercial Clients providing bespoke video and media services including working with a National Charity and a regional property developer. We also specialise in internet promos and specialist social media content. 
Events Productions
Newland Studios have recorded numerous private and public events on behalf of both individuals and company clients.  these range from Stage Shows, Concerts, Parties and Weddings. 
Specialist  Performance A/V
We are also experienced in  creating specialist media for  live productions, having done work for  Hull Truck, Hull New Theatre, Northern Academy and Kardomah94.  These have ranged from simple projected backgrounds to full interactive scenes and incidental videos.
Music Promo Videos
Newland Studios has worked with numerous performing artists to provide them with promotional materials. we have worked with both single performers and bands, both using live performances and recorded music. Whatever your requirements you can be sure we can accomodate you.
